S.E.M. STUDY

All samples were found to consist of quartz and calcite grains with a high percentage of mixed clays, differing only in the relative proportions of the constituents.

A had the highest proportion of quartz with fairly low carbonates and a high mixed clay content.

B had a high percentage of carbonates, mainly Ca, with some Mg; low quartz content and fairly high clay content.

C consisted almost entirely of clays with minor amounts of quartz and carbonates.

EDAX analysis showed all samples to contain the following elements:-
Si, Al, Na, Mg, K, Ca, Fe, Ba and Cl.

At the time of analysis no information was available on the drilling mud composition and consequently it was not possible to determine if any of these elements arose from mud damage.
