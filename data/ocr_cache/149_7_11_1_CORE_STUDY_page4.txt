Enclosed are the inferred environments and relative rates of sedimentations you wanted. In the absence of any population estimates, I have based the sedimentation rates on the number of species listed, and the environments on the presence of certain facies markers. Material caving from above could cause some of the determinations to be erroneous, but the Robertson staff seems to have eliminated much of this caved material from their consideration or there was only an insignificant amount.

Some of the species reported I was unfamiliar with and had no literature on them. A knowledge of these species could alter a few of the determinations, but overall, this knowledge would only confirm.

In the Pleistocene to Middle Miocene, the determinations indicated could be inaccurate. These sediments may be masked VII (or VIII?). The shallow water fossils could be displaced faunas.

The facies and relative rates of sedimentation are marked on the Robertson Analysis Chart.

Walter
