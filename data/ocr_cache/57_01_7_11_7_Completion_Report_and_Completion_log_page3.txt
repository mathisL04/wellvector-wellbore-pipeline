I. OBJECTIVES

1. To report and evaluate the data obtained during the drilling and testing of Cod 7/11-7 slot 6.

2. Evaluate the hydrocarbon potential of the three exploration zones penetrated by the wellbore.

II. CONCLUSIONS

1. The drilling and testing of Cod 7/11-7 was a qualified success. The project has demonstrated the presence of hydrocarbons (Oil API 41.1 deg) in the Jurassic and Upper Triassic aged sandstones at high temperatures (360 deg F) high pressures (>11,000 PSI) and deep penetration (14,000 - 15,000 ft md).

2. A lengthy testing program on three separate formations established the presence of gas and oil but due to either poor rock quality or mechanical problems, the find is not presently commercial.

3. It is probable that the mud program damaged the reservoir rock in the near wellbore extensively which in turn adversely affected the test flowrates.

III. RECOMMENDATIONS

1. Drill to the Triassic/Jurassic age sandstones again at locations directed by Geology.
