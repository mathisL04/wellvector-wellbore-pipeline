This test probably covered several separate reservoirs which can be suspected to exist because of shale breaks but which remain undefinable at present. DST No. 1 covered a tight section, the equivalent of which was not tested in 7/11-1X, but could be productive at other well locations on the structure with lateral variation in porosity.

The results of preliminary sand studies on cores and drill cuttings of the Paleocene sandstones in which it is concluded that deposition was by turbidity currents in deep marine water is an added complication for definition of sand distribution over the structure as well as for definition of reserves. Prediction of the direction of optimum thickness and porosity of sand members on the Cod structure is not possible with the available data of the two wells.

The data from 7/11-1X and 7/11-2X provide nothing to change the geophysical interpretation of the size and shape of the Cod prospect.
